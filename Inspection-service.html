Maritime inspection and survey services overview

Eldawisons offer a wide range of inspection and survey services, carried out by our experts. We help you in monitoring and identifying any potential threats, along with providing environment-friendly solutions for shipping industry. By this approach we ensure that your vessel operates efficiently.

Eldawisons inspection services

We strive to meet the highest safety and regulatory standards in shipping industry through providing high-quality maritime inspection services. Our goal is to make sure that your vessel is in its optimum condition and equipped with essential maritime supplies, ensuring safe sea voyages. The services we provide include:

  • • Supplying, installing, and testing safety equipment: we provide annual inspection and monitoring for critical maritime supplies such as; life-saving appliances, personal protective gears, and fire safety.

  • • Maritime crew’s personal safety equipment Inspection: this includes the inspection and maintenance of life and rescue boats, along with associated rescue equipment such as life rafts. By this approach, we ensure that vessels are compliant with SOLAS (safety of life at sea) requirements.

  • • Maritime Life-saving devices inspection: lifebuoys and jackets, immersion suits, breathing apparatus, and IMO safety signs are all monitored, ensuring crew’s safety in emergency situations.

  • • Fire safety equipment: Our maritime inspection services also include the regular check and maintenance of fire extinguishers such as; foam, dry chemical, and CO2.

Marine operations & ship supplies survey services

At eldawisons, we help you to be always aware of any potential issues through our professional maritime survey services. We offer comprehensive evaluations of various vessel components and cargo operations that include:

  • • Cargo surveys and inspection: we ensure efficient cargo handling, storage, and compliance with marine regulations through offering full assessments and detailed surveys for you cargo operations.

  • • Tally surveys for cargos: to ensure proper documentation and prevent cargo loss.

  • • Cargo condition surveys: to provide all the details about the condition of goods in transit, and whether or not a damage or deterioration happened during the marine journey.

  • • Cargo loss surveys: this type of surveys offer a complete assessments of any potential or actual cargo losses, identifying causes and giving straightforward solutions for mitigation.

  • • Damage surveys: help in determining the scope and impact of any damages occurred to the ships or cargos.

  • • Hull and machinery surveys: refers to the reports resulted from routine inspections of the ship’s hull, and engines to guarantee proper functioning and full compliance with safety regulations.

  • • Drafts survey: it include accurate measurements for the ship drafts in order to determine the cargo weight and ensure that the ship is balanced.

  • • Bunker surveys: ensuring that vessel’s fuel supply is in its optimum condition.

  • • Tank cleanliness survey: verification of the cleanliness of tanks and holds, specifically for sensitive cargos such as; chemicals or food-grade products.

  • • Marine contractor’s supervision: it ensures timely projects completion and compliance with marine regulations.
