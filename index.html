Who We Are?

ElDawi Sons marine services is an Egyptian company established in 1975 by Mr. Mohamed ElDawi and presently co-operated by Mr. Moamen ElDawi . We offer diversified services in the maritime industry with numerous highly trained employees and team of experts so professionalism and quality is guaranteed.

Our Mission

We are keen to provide customer satisfaction by meeting needs in a timely and effective manner, believing this can be achieved by empowering our workforce with high trainings and applying innovative tactics to expand and integrate international management systems and an advanced environment

Our Vision

We aspire to be your first choice , your go to company. Growing in the region by focusing on leveraging our core strengths. Accessibility of operating offices in the ports of the routes we serve so we can provide all our services to you anywhere. Improve communities and environments in which we operate. Excel and lead in the industry!
