Overview of general ship supply services

At Eldawisons, we specialize in providing all the ship supplies including maritime provisions, cabin and safety stores, technical and maintenance supplies, and anti-piracy equipment. By offering high-quality products, customized quotes, and diverse credit payment options, we ensure that vessels are well-equipped for safe and long voyages.

Main ship supplies categories:
1-provisions and bonded stores

We take the whole responsibility towards meeting the daily requirements of your vessel. We offer a wide collection of maritime provisions including fresh, dry, and frozen provisions local and imported with refrigeration and freezing facilities.

  • • Fresh provisions: daily deliveries of fruits, vegetables, dairy, and more.
  • • Dry and frozen goods: to meet different dietary needs for the crew in vessels.
  • • Bonded stores: duty-free goods and essential supplies vessels in transit.

Fresh Provisions

2-Technical Stores

At eldawisons, we’re totally aware of the importance of vessels maintenance and repairs. Our stores are stocked with essential supplies that fulfill the various needs of vessels, ensuring optimum condition and reducing their down time. Our maritime technical stores include:

  • • Engine supplies: quality engine parts, lubricants, and accessories
  • • Mooring & towing ropes.
  • • Galvanized and non-galvanized Steel wire ropes and its accessories.
  • • Anchors, anchor chains, shackles and lashing equipment.
  • • Marine chemicals and tanks cleaning materials.
  • • Coating, paint and its equipment.
  • • All types of bolts, nuts, washers, and valves.
  • • Carbon steel pipes tubes and its fittings.


3-Cabin, safety, and stationary stores

In order to guarantee that all vessels are safe and compliant with maritime regulations, we offer comprehensive range of cabin supplies, and safety equipment. Items that come under this category include:

  • • Personal protective equipment (PPE): such as helmets, gloves, life-jackets...etc.
  • • Firefighting equipment: marine-grade firefighting systems, extinguishers, and protective gears
  • • Safety, IMO signs and rescue equipment.
  • • Hoses, coupling, and nozzles: for marine and industrial use.
  • • Pneumatic and electrical tools: including decaling, grinding Equipment, pumps and winches.
  • • Hand and cutting tools & measuring instruments.
  • • Packing and jointing materials.
  • • Welding and cutting equipment.

  • • Barbed wire

  • • Secure fence

5-catering services

Eldawisons is dedicated to meet the dietary needs of marine crew, providing them with nutritious and delicious food to ease their long sea voyages. Our goal is to endure their safety, enhance their well-being, and bring enjoyment to their journey no matter how challenging the circumstances. Eldawisons stands out for:

Marine supply chain that covers all Egyptian ports: With our extensive supply chain spanning all Egyptian ports, we ensure seamless sourcing of both crew and supplies for vessels, regardless of the vessel’s destination or origin.. By this approach eldawisons, saves you both time and money efficiently.

Bursting taste and minimized waste: Our professional crew has a wide experience in providing esteemed catering services characterized by both sustainability and delicious tastes. We excel in crafting high-quality menu cycles featuring diverse and nutritious food options, ensuring the crew’s nutritious needs are met while keeping meals exciting and free from monotony. Additionally, eldawisons catering prioritizes the minimization of wastes in both food and money through innovative menu planning and meal preparation practices.
